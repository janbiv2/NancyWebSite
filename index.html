About Homeward Bound

As a fellow pet lover who has owned pets my entire life, I understand how important it is for your pets to feel loved and cared for during your absence. With over 10 years of experience providing in-home pet care for my clients, you can have the peace of mind you and your pets expect while you are away.

I specialize in caring for dogs (all breeds), cats, rabbits, guinea pigs and other small animals. I will conduct a home visit prior to your vacation date so your pet(s) can become acclimated to a new person.

Services

My ultimate goal is to provide your pets with a routine they are both comfortable and familiar with, making the transition during your absence as easy as possible. This is not an exhaustive list - if you do not see a service your pet(s) require, please contact me to see if that service can be arranged.

  • Home visits are approximately 30 to 40 minutes in length.
  • Feed all animals and refill water bowls.
  • Exercise pets in yard or walk around neighborhood.
  • Clean dog waste and change litter boxes.
  • Have play time and provide love and affection for all pets.
  • Collect mail, newspapers, and bring in garbage barrels.
  • Turn lights on/off so home does not appear empty.
  • Administer medication if necessary.
  • Communicate with pet owner regarding any issues while you are away.

We Come to You!

Having your pet stay at home in a familiar environment typically causes less stress for your animals while you are away. Leaving your pet at home with a caring professional such as Homeward Bound Pet Sitting ensures even less stress for you! I will always treat your home, your pets, and the responsibility of caring for both seriously, professionally and lovingly.
